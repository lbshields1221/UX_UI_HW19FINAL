SAMHSA, Government Website Redesign

COMPASS CONNECTIONS, Non-Profit Redesign

Due to the rebranding of BCFS Health and Human Services, it was observed that the current solution, Compass Connections isn’t meeting the current needs of foster parents. When users try to find resources, to guide the fostering process, they either can’t find the website, or they don’t know where to click to complete the registration process.
